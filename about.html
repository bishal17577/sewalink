Our Mission

To create a transparent bridge between citizens and leaders, enabling efficient reporting and resolution of local development issues across Nepal. We believe that every voice matters and every problem deserves attention.

Our Story

Develop Nepal was born from a simple observation: citizens had no direct way to report local issues to their leaders. Problems like road damage, water shortages, and infrastructure failures would go unnoticed for months.

Founded by Bishal Shrestha from Dhading, our platform bridges this gap. We provide a simple, transparent way for citizens to report issues and for leaders to track, accept, and resolve them.

Today, we're proud to serve communities across all 7 provinces of Nepal, with hundreds of issues resolved and dozens of active leaders participating.
