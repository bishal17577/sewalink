Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 7, 2026

At Develop Nepal, accessible from developnepal.com.np, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Develop Nepal and how we use it.

Information We Collect

When you register on our platform, we may ask you to provide certain personal information, including:

  • Name and email address (via Google Sign-In)
  • Profile information you choose to share
  • Issues and content you post
  • Comments and interactions on the platform

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Notify you about changes to our platform
  • Allow you to participate in interactive features
  • Provide customer support
  • Monitor usage of our platform
  • Detect and prevent technical issues

Google Sign-In

We use Google Sign-In as our sole authentication method. When you sign in with Google, we receive:

  • Your name and email address
  • Profile picture (if available)

We do not store your Google password. All authentication is handled securely by Google.

Image Uploads

Images uploaded to our platform are stored securely via ImgBB API. These images are:

  • Stored on secure servers
  • Used only for the intended purpose of displaying issues
  • Not shared with third parties

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your login session
  • Understand your preferences
  • Analyze platform usage

Third Party Privacy Policies

Develop Nepal's Privacy Policy does not apply to other advertisers or websites. We advise you to consult the respective Privacy Policies of these third-party services for more information.

Your Data Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Children's Information

Our platform does not knowingly collect any personal identifiable information from children under the age of 13. If you think that your child provided this kind of information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
